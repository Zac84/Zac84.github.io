Personal reflections on the Ethical issues relating to Ai Advances, Capabilites, and Limits

Artificial Intelligence will provide developments that will be ethically positive, negative, neutral, mixed, and ambiguous. Some AI technologies will be of multiple uses. An example could be, any AI program that can be used to identify marine life could also be used for targeting a marine ecosystem. With multiple adjustments added to the software, the drones could be re-targeted for other creatures, even humans.
